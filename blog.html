Information about Tigers

Common Name: Tiger
Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris
Genus: Panthera
Species: Tigris

Habitat

The tiger is a big cat mostly found in various parts of the world. Among the various species of tigers, the largest one Amur Tiger, also known as Siberian, Northern tiger is found in the Siberian, Northern Regions. Another popular species of tiger is the Royal Bengal Tiger which is typically found in the South Asian region. The tiger is a very strong and ferocious beast that is capable of knocking the winds off its enemy with a single blow. The tiger is an apex predator that usually lives in solitude. This magestical beast is one of the most beloved wild animals. In Nepal, The tiger is found in various national parks like the Chitwan National Park, Bardiya National Park etc.
